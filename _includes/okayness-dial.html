{%- comment -%} Okayness dial — speedometer-style SVG widget. Layout (degrees measured clockwise from 12 o'clock): Terrible (1) → 225° (bottom-left, red) Bad (2) → 270° (9 o'clock) Not Okay (3) → 315° (top-left) Neutral (4) → 0° (top) Okay (5) → 45° (top-right) Good (6) → 90° (3 o'clock) Great (7) → 135° (bottom-right, green) Numb (0) → 180° (straight down, off the arc) Needle angle = 45 × score − 180. For s=1 → −135° (bottom-left), s=4 → 0° (top), s=7 → 135° (bottom-right).
